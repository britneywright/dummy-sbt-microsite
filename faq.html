Where is right-biased Either?

Up to Cats 0.6.x we had cats.data.Xor which was effectively scala.util.Either, but right-biased by default and with a bunch of useful combinators around it. In Scala 2.12.x Either became right-biased so we revisited the use of Xor and decided that in the interest of interoperability we move to scala.util.Either and fill in the gaps via syntax enrichment.

This syntax can be imported via cats.syntax.either._ or through cats.implicits._.

Similarly, cats.data.XorT has been replaced with cats.data.EitherT.
